What beginners should listen for

Beat basics

Snare on 2 and 4. Keep kick notes simple so the groove stays easy to feel.

R&B and soul

Use fewer notes, lighter hats, and small velocity changes so the groove sounds relaxed instead of busy.

Neo soul

Let the snare sit deeper in the pocket, add syncopated hats, and leave space around the kick.
